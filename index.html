About

“The best programs are written so that computing machines can perform them quickly and so that human beings can understand them clearly. A programmer is ideally an essayist who works with traditional aesthetic and literary forms as well as mathematical concepts, to communicate the way that an algorithm works and to convince a reader that the results will be correct.”
- Donald E. Knuth, Selected Papers on Computer Science

Programming is an outlet for me to showcase my creativity and desire to solve complex problems. For me, there's nothing more fullfilling then to create a product that is useful and brings joy to the people who use it. I look at development as art, with the keyboard being my brush and the code editor as my canvas.

I have created everything from E-commerce sites for small startup companies to websites and applications for multimillion dollar companies.

My skill set includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Redux, NodeJS, C#, .NET, SQL, and Firebase
